Our Story

How We Met...

Priya and Rohan's story began not with a spark, but with a shared textbook. As fate would have it, they were both enrolled in the same intimidating statistics class during their second year of college. Priya, ever-organized, had the last copy of the required book at the library, and Rohan, characteristically last-minute, was desperately searching for it. A polite request turned into a study session, and study sessions turned into late-night coffee dates filled with laughter and shared dreams.

Years later, on a quiet evening at the very same college library where they first met, Rohan got down on one knee. He had bookmarked a page in a statistics book, and instead of formulas, a simple question was written: "Will you be my partner, forever?" With tears of joy, Priya said yes, marking the beginning of their next great chapter.
